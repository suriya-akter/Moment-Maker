Our Services

wedding Planner

Your wedding should be a time of joy, not stress. Our experienced wedding planners will take care of every detail – vendor coordination, timeline management, budgeting, and those inevitable hiccups – so you can focus on celebrating this momentous occasion with your loved ones.
We believe that no two weddings should be alike. Whether you envision a classic, elegant affair, a whimsical outdoor ceremony, or a vibrant destination wedding, we'll help bring your personal vision to life. Our customized planning packages are designed to fit your unique needs and style.

Package Includes

 

  1. PWE Weddings & Events Workbook
  2. Unlimited access to expert wedding advice (via e-mail)
  3. Access to Preferred Vendors List
  4. Wedding Assistant for wedding day
  5. Oversee and help with ceremony/reception site set up & tear down
  6. Review of Vendor contracts
  7. Distribute final payments and gratuities to vendors as necessary
